Jalanthar

Personal project

Three years of running a homebrew D&D campaign set in the city of Jalanthar — writing the world, managing a cast of 60+ NPCs, planning sessions weeks in advance, and building a custom web app to track all of it. What started as notes in a notebook eventually needed its own infrastructure.

The app — the Jalanthar Player's Chronicle — is a React + Firebase tool I built to replace 200 pages of scattered notes. It gives players an interactive map of the city, a browsable NPC relationship graph, and a building catalog with in-world inventory. On the DM side, I control what's visible: NPCs with visible: false are blocked at the Firestore security rules level, not just hidden in the UI. It's live and playable below.
